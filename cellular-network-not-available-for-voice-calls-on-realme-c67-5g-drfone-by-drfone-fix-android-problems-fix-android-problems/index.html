2. Terrain Coverage

Another contributing factor is the geographical terrain. In remote or sparsely populated areas, cellular tower coverage might be limited. It causes a signal disturbance or complete loss of the network.

3. Network Congestion

During peak usage hours or in densely populated areas, network congestion can also be a cause. It leads to temporary unavailability of the cellular network for voice calls. Furthermore, technical glitches within the Realme C67 5G device or network infrastructure can cause this issue. These glitches could range from software bugs to hardware malfunctions.

In some instances, insufficient battery power or power-saving modes on devices can cause this issue. They affect the Realme C67 5G device’s capacity to sustain a consistent connection with the cellular network. This consequently causes voice call unavailability.

Soft Brick

Soft brick is like a temporary setback for your device. To see if your Poco C50 is soft - bricked, check out the details below:

  • It happens when the software encounters issues, but the hardware is usually fine.
  • Your device might not start properly, freeze, or get stuck on the logo screen.

Now, this is a more severe situation. In this case, your bricked Poco C50 might fall under the following conditions:

  • Thesoftware and hardware have significant problems, making the Poco C50 device unresponsive.

A custom ROM is like a customized version of your device’s software. While it can bring cool new features, it can also cause issues if not installed correctly. Incompatible or poorly made custom ROMs can sometimes lead to a bricked device.

If you’re trying to tweak your device’s software without proper knowledge, you might make changes that the Poco C50 device can’t handle. This can lead to instability and, you guessed it, a bricked device.

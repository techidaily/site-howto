3. Should the first two steps do not work, charge your OnePlus Nord N30 5G to get it out of the start-up loop. You can also opt to use a different battery, just in case your current battery is the source of the problem.

5. Start your OnePlus Nord N30 5G in **Safe Mode** by pressing and holding down the **Menu** or **Volume Down** button on your device.

A touch screen not working on Oppo Find X7 smartphones indicates a soft brick (recoverable by firmware update), often caused by a defective or undercharged battery. Screens on Oppo Find X7 devices may become unresponsive for a variety of hardware-related reasons. A broken LCD panel, faulty touch sensor, or loosened ribbon wires are all possible causes. Every Oppo Find X7 device, including the Note 4 and 5, is susceptible to these problems.

The Android operating system is adjustable. Some of our technological advancements may backfire on us. The phone won't power on or become stuck in a boot loop where the logo will only show intermittently.

1. **Enabling Airplane mode**

**Why is My ZTE Axon 40 Lite showing offline**? Well, one common culprit behind the "offline" message is the accidental activation of Airplane Mode on your ZTE Axon 40 Lite. Fluctuations in your signal strength or unexpected data loss can also be the culprit behind the **"Why is My ZTE Axon 40 Lite offline?"** problem. Android devices rely on stable internet connections, and disruptions, whether temporary or prolonged, can lead to this error.

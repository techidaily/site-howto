- **During Use/Gaming:** The screen may become unresponsive due to overheating when using or gaming on a Realme C33 2023 phone. This can be caused by running too many apps simultaneously or playing games for an extended period, especially on models such as the Realme C33 2023 9 Pro, which generates much heat during gaming.

- **No Response When Touching the Screen:**Sometimes, the Realme C33 2023 screen may not respond when touched due to a faulty touch screen or display. This can happen due to physical damage or a manufacturing defect. Some Realme C33 2023 Nord 2 users have reported this issue.

- **After Dropping the Phone:** If a Realme C33 2023 phone has been dropped, the screen may become unresponsive due to physical damage or a loose connection. This can happen to any model, including the Realme C33 2023.

Overheating, physical damage, and manufacturing defects are some common causes of Realme C33 2023 screen unresponsiveness, which may occur during use/gaming, no response when touching the screen, or after dropping the phone.

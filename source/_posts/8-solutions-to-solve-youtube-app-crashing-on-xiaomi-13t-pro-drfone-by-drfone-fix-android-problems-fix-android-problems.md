## What causes the Xiaomi 13T Pro Screen unresponsive?

The Xiaomi 13T Pro screen may become unresponsive due to various reasons. Here are some common situations and their possible causes:

- **During Use/Gaming:** The screen may become unresponsive due to overheating when using or gaming on a Xiaomi 13T Pro phone. This can be caused by running too many apps simultaneously or playing games for an extended period, especially on models such as the Xiaomi 13T Pro 9 Pro, which generates much heat during gaming.

- **No Response When Touching the Screen:**Sometimes, the Xiaomi 13T Pro screen may not respond when touched due to a faulty touch screen or display. This can happen due to physical damage or a manufacturing defect. Some Xiaomi 13T Pro Nord 2 users have reported this issue.

- **After Dropping the Phone:** If a Xiaomi 13T Pro phone has been dropped, the screen may become unresponsive due to physical damage or a loose connection. This can happen to any model, including the Xiaomi 13T Pro.

Overheating, physical damage, and manufacturing defects are some common causes of Xiaomi 13T Pro screen unresponsiveness, which may occur during use/gaming, no response when touching the screen, or after dropping the phone.

## Part 1: What might cause the bootloop issue on Android?

Android boot loop error might seem weird and unexplainable but it occurs because of some specific reasons.

In a rooted device, changes made, such as flashing a new ROM or customized firmware which are not compatible with the Xiaomi 13T Pro device’s hardware or existing software, can be blamed for the boot loop problem.

Moving on, when your device’s software is unable to communicate with the system files during the start-up process, Android boot loop problem may arise. Such a glitch is caused if you have recently updated the Android version.

Also, corrupt App update files may also cause the bootloop Android issue. Apps and programs downloaded from unknown sources bring in a particular type of virus that prevents you from using your device smoothly.

All-in-all, Android boot loop error is a direct outcome of when you try to tamper with your device’s internal settings.

## Part 4: Boot into Safe Mode to fix Android boot loop

![boot into safe mode](https://images.wondershare.com/drfone/article/2023/08/boot-loop-android.jpg)

Booting your Xiaomi 13T Pro into Safe Mode is a useful troubleshooting step to fix boot loop issues caused by third-party apps. When in Safe Mode, the Xiaomi 13T Pro device will start up with only essential system apps, and any downloaded apps that might be causing conflicts won't run. Here's how you can boot your Xiaomi 13T Pro into Safe Mode to address the boot loop problem:

**Step 1: Power Off Your Device**

Hold down the power button until it turns off completely.

**Step 2: Access Safe Mode**

To enter Safe Mode, follow these steps based on your device model:

- For most Android devices: Press and hold the power button to turn on your device. As soon as the manufacturer's logo appears, press and hold the Volume Down button until the Xiaomi 13T Pro device finishes booting. You should see "Safe Mode" displayed in the bottom left or right corner of the screen.
- Some devices might require slightly different key combinations. Refer to your device's user manual or perform an online search for the specific method to enter Safe Mode for your model.

**Step 3: Observe the Xiaomi 13T Pro device**

Once your device has booted into Safe Mode, use it for a while to see if the boot loop issue persists. If the boot loop doesn't occur in Safe Mode, it indicates that the problem is likely caused by a third-party app.

**Step 4: Identify and Uninstall Problematic Apps**

After each uninstallation, restart your device normally to check if the boot loop still occurs. Continue this process until you find the problematic app.

**Step 5: Restart Normally**

Once you've identified and uninstalled the problematic app, restart your device normally to exit Safe Mode. Your Android device should now start up without getting stuck in a boot loop.

By following these steps and using Safe Mode to isolate problematic apps, you can effectively troubleshoot and resolve boot loop issues on your Xiaomi 13T Pro.

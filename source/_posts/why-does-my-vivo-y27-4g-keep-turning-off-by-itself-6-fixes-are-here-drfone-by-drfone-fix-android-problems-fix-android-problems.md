- **During Use/Gaming:** The screen may become unresponsive due to overheating when using or gaming on a Vivo Y27 4G phone. - **No Response When Touching the Screen:**Sometimes, the Vivo Y27 4G screen may not respond when touched due to a faulty touch screen or display. This can happen due to physical damage or a manufacturing defect. Some Vivo Y27 4G Nord 2 users have reported this issue.

Overheating, physical damage, and manufacturing defects are some common causes of Vivo Y27 4G screen unresponsiveness, which may occur during use/gaming, no response when touching the screen, or after dropping the phone.

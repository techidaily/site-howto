## What causes the Lava Blaze 2 Screen unresponsive?

The Lava Blaze 2 screen may become unresponsive due to various reasons. Here are some common situations and their possible causes:

- **During Use/Gaming:** The screen may become unresponsive due to overheating when using or gaming on a Lava Blaze 2 phone. This can be caused by running too many apps simultaneously or playing games for an extended period, especially on models such as the Lava Blaze 2 9 Pro, which generates much heat during gaming.

- **No Response When Touching the Screen:**Sometimes, the Lava Blaze 2 screen may not respond when touched due to a faulty touch screen or display. This can happen due to physical damage or a manufacturing defect. Some Lava Blaze 2 Nord 2 users have reported this issue.

- **After Dropping the Phone:** If a Lava Blaze 2 phone has been dropped, the screen may become unresponsive due to physical damage or a loose connection. This can happen to any model, including the Lava Blaze 2.

Overheating, physical damage, and manufacturing defects are some common causes of Lava Blaze 2 screen unresponsiveness, which may occur during use/gaming, no response when touching the screen, or after dropping the phone.

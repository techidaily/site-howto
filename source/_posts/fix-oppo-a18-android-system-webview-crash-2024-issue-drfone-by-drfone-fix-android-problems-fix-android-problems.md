Slow loading times can also be a sign of an impending crash. If webpages within the app take unusually long to load or images and other content appear pixelated or distorted, it could be a sign that Oppo A18 Android System WebView is struggling to handle the workload.

Oppo A18 Android System WebView requires a certain amount of memory to operate properly. If your device is running low on memory, it can cause Oppo A18 Android System WebView to crash. It is more likely to happen on older devices with less RAM.

WebView might crash if it conflicts with other resources or processes running on the Oppo A18 device. It can happen if the Oppo A18 device runs low on memory or other resource-intensive apps compete for system resources.

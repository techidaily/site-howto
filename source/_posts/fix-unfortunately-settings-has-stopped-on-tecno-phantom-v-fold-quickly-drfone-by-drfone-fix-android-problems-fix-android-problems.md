## Part 1: Clear the cache of Settings and Google Play Service

It is possible that the corrupted cache files are responsible for this error. 1. Open “Settings” on your Tecno Phantom V Fold and choose “Apps & Notifications”/”Apps”/”Application Manager” (the option may differ on different devices).

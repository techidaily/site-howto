### 2.1 Open the Phone app in Safe Mode

First and foremost, the thing that can let you get rid of this trouble is Safe Mode. It is a feature that will end any excessive background functioning of the Honor Magic 5 device. For instance, your device will be able to run sans any third-party apps when in Safe mode. Since the important functions and the naïve apps will be running on the Honor Magic 5 device, you will get to know if it’s really a software glitch or not by running the Phone app in Safe mode. 2. Now keep pressing the "Power" button until you see the Honor Magic 5 logo on the screen.

4. Leave the key once the Honor Magic 5 device is in Safe mode. Now, the third-party apps will be disabled and you can check if the Phone app is still not responding or everything is fine.

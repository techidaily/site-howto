But why does WebView crash Android smartphones? The answer involves several factors:

Xiaomi 13 Ultra Android System WebView requires a certain amount of memory to operate properly. If your device is running low on memory, it can cause Xiaomi 13 Ultra Android System WebView to crash. It is more likely to happen on older devices with less RAM.

- **Conflicts With Other Apps**

WebView might crash if it conflicts with other resources or processes running on the Xiaomi 13 Ultra device. It can happen if the Xiaomi 13 Ultra device runs low on memory or other resource-intensive apps compete for system resources.

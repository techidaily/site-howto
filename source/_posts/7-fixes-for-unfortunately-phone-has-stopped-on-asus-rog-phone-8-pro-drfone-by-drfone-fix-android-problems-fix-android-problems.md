First and foremost, the thing that can let you get rid of this trouble is Safe Mode. It is a feature that will end any excessive background functioning of the Asus ROG Phone 8 Pro device. 3. Release the button and immediately press and hold the "Volume Down" key.
4. Leave the key once the Asus ROG Phone 8 Pro device is in Safe mode. Now, the third-party apps will be disabled and you can check if the Phone app is still not responding or everything is fine.

Vivo Y200 Android System WebView requires a certain amount of memory to operate properly. If your device is running low on memory, it can cause Vivo Y200 Android System WebView to crash. It is more likely to happen on older devices with less RAM.

WebView might crash if it conflicts with other resources or processes running on the Vivo Y200 device. It can happen if the Vivo Y200 device runs low on memory or other resource-intensive apps compete for system resources.

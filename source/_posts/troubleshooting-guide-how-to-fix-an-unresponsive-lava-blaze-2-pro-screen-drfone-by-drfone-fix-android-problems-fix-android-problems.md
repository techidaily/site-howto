## What causes the Lava Blaze 2 Pro Screen unresponsive?

The Lava Blaze 2 Pro screen may become unresponsive due to various reasons. Here are some common situations and their possible causes:

- **During Use/Gaming:** The screen may become unresponsive due to overheating when using or gaming on a Lava Blaze 2 Pro phone. This can be caused by running too many apps simultaneously or playing games for an extended period, especially on models such as the Lava Blaze 2 Pro 9 Pro, which generates much heat during gaming.

- **No Response When Touching the Screen:**Sometimes, the Lava Blaze 2 Pro screen may not respond when touched due to a faulty touch screen or display. This can happen due to physical damage or a manufacturing defect. Some Lava Blaze 2 Pro Nord 2 users have reported this issue.

- **After Dropping the Phone:** If a Lava Blaze 2 Pro phone has been dropped, the screen may become unresponsive due to physical damage or a loose connection. This can happen to any model, including the Lava Blaze 2 Pro.

Overheating, physical damage, and manufacturing defects are some common causes of Lava Blaze 2 Pro screen unresponsiveness, which may occur during use/gaming, no response when touching the screen, or after dropping the phone.

## The common solutions to solve a Lava Blaze 2 Pro Screen unresponsive

### 2.2 Ensure the SIM card is not dirty or wet

### 2.3 Insert the SIM card correctly

If your SIM card was working properly until yet, there is a good possibility that the SIM card may have moved a bit from its actual location. Eventually, there is poor contact between the SIM card pins and the circuit. Try inserting your SIM card properly with the following steps.

- Power off your Lava Blaze 2 Pro and with the help of Q pin, eject the SIM card holder from the SIM slot of your device.
- Now, grab a soft rubber pencil eraser and gently rub it in on the gold pins of SIM card to clean them properly. Then, with the help of a soft cloth wipe away the rubber residue from the SIM card.
- Next, push the SIM back into the SIM card holder properly and push it back into the SIM slot now.
- Turn your device back on and look if your SIM not provisioned on the Android issue is resolved or not.

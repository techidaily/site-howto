## What causes the ZTE Nubia Z60 Ultra Screen unresponsive?

The ZTE Nubia Z60 Ultra screen may become unresponsive due to various reasons. Here are some common situations and their possible causes:

- **During Use/Gaming:** The screen may become unresponsive due to overheating when using or gaming on a ZTE Nubia Z60 Ultra phone. This can be caused by running too many apps simultaneously or playing games for an extended period, especially on models such as the ZTE Nubia Z60 Ultra 9 Pro, which generates much heat during gaming.

- **No Response When Touching the Screen:**Sometimes, the ZTE Nubia Z60 Ultra screen may not respond when touched due to a faulty touch screen or display. This can happen due to physical damage or a manufacturing defect. Some ZTE Nubia Z60 Ultra Nord 2 users have reported this issue.

- **After Dropping the Phone:** If a ZTE Nubia Z60 Ultra phone has been dropped, the screen may become unresponsive due to physical damage or a loose connection. This can happen to any model, including the ZTE Nubia Z60 Ultra.

Overheating, physical damage, and manufacturing defects are some common causes of ZTE Nubia Z60 Ultra screen unresponsiveness, which may occur during use/gaming, no response when touching the screen, or after dropping the phone.

## The common solutions to solve a ZTE Nubia Z60 Ultra Screen unresponsive

**Step 2:** Try a different charger or cable to see if the issue is with the charging accessories.

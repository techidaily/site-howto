Step 1 – Tap and hold the power button of your Motorola Moto G13.

Step 2 – You could find the option “Restart”. Tap on it. (If you have only one option, move to the step no 2)

Step 3 – Now, your phone will boot up in some time and you can see the Motorola Moto G13 device is no more on safe mode.

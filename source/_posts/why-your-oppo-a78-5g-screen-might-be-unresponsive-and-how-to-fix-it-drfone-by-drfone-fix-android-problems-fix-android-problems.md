## Common Causes of Oppo A78 5G Screen Unresponsiveness

Over time, the touch screen of an Oppo A78 5G device may become unresponsive due to wear and tear. This can happen to models like the Oppo A78 5G F1s and F3. In this case, replacing the touch screen or seeking professional assistance may be necessary.

Dropping or impacting an Oppo A78 5G device can cause physical damage to the touch screen, resulting in unresponsiveness. This can happen to various models like the Oppo A78 5G Reno and Find X. In some cases, the damage may be repairable, but the screen replacement or the Oppo A78 5G device itself may be necessary in severe cases. Protecting the Oppo A78 5G device with a case and handling it carefully to prevent physical damage is important.

While Oppo A78 5G devices are generally reliable, various factors can contribute to screen unresponsiveness. Users can effectively troubleshoot and resolve issues by identifying these potential causes and seeking appropriate solutions.

5. Start your Oppo A78 5G in **Safe Mode** by pressing and holding down the **Menu** or **Volume Down** button on your device.

## What causes the OnePlus Ace 2 Pro Screen unresponsive?

The OnePlus Ace 2 Pro screen may become unresponsive due to various reasons. Here are some common situations and their possible causes:

- **During Use/Gaming:** The screen may become unresponsive due to overheating when using or gaming on a OnePlus Ace 2 Pro phone. This can be caused by running too many apps simultaneously or playing games for an extended period, especially on models such as the OnePlus Ace 2 Pro 9 Pro, which generates much heat during gaming.

- **No Response When Touching the Screen:**Sometimes, the OnePlus Ace 2 Pro screen may not respond when touched due to a faulty touch screen or display. This can happen due to physical damage or a manufacturing defect. Some OnePlus Ace 2 Pro Nord 2 users have reported this issue.

- **After Dropping the Phone:** If a OnePlus Ace 2 Pro phone has been dropped, the screen may become unresponsive due to physical damage or a loose connection. This can happen to any model, including the OnePlus Ace 2 Pro.

Overheating, physical damage, and manufacturing defects are some common causes of OnePlus Ace 2 Pro screen unresponsiveness, which may occur during use/gaming, no response when touching the screen, or after dropping the phone.

**Step 1:** Plug the phone into a charger and wait for at least 30 minutes to see if the battery charges.

**Step 2:** Try a different charger or cable to see if the issue is with the charging accessories.

**Step 1:** Go to **Settings** \> **Battery** > **Battery Temperature.**

**Step 2:** If the phone is overheating, let it cool down before using it again.

**Step 1:** Go to **Settings** \> **Additional Settings** > **Backup and Reset** > **Erase All Data**.

**Step 2:** Follow the on-screen instructions to reset the phone.

![realme new screen](https://images.wondershare.com/drfone/article/2023/03/realme-touch-screen-not-working-6.jpg)

## What causes the Asus ROG Phone 7 Ultimate Screen unresponsive?

The Asus ROG Phone 7 Ultimate screen may become unresponsive due to various reasons. Here are some common situations and their possible causes:

- **During Use/Gaming:** The screen may become unresponsive due to overheating when using or gaming on a Asus ROG Phone 7 Ultimate phone. This can be caused by running too many apps simultaneously or playing games for an extended period, especially on models such as the Asus ROG Phone 7 Ultimate 9 Pro, which generates much heat during gaming.

- **No Response When Touching the Screen:**Sometimes, the Asus ROG Phone 7 Ultimate screen may not respond when touched due to a faulty touch screen or display. This can happen due to physical damage or a manufacturing defect. Some Asus ROG Phone 7 Ultimate Nord 2 users have reported this issue.

- **After Dropping the Phone:** If a Asus ROG Phone 7 Ultimate phone has been dropped, the screen may become unresponsive due to physical damage or a loose connection. This can happen to any model, including the Asus ROG Phone 7 Ultimate.

Overheating, physical damage, and manufacturing defects are some common causes of Asus ROG Phone 7 Ultimate screen unresponsiveness, which may occur during use/gaming, no response when touching the screen, or after dropping the phone.

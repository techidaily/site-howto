- **No Response When Touching the Screen:**Sometimes, the Xiaomi Redmi K70 screen may not respond when touched due to a faulty touch screen or display. This can happen due to physical damage or a manufacturing defect. Some Xiaomi Redmi K70 Nord 2 users have reported this issue.

Overheating, physical damage, and manufacturing defects are some common causes of Xiaomi Redmi K70 screen unresponsiveness, which may occur during use/gaming, no response when touching the screen, or after dropping the phone.
